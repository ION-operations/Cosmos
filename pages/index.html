Design Brief

A renderer with receipts.

Cosmos follows the ION project style: visible evidence before claims, stable IDs for repeatable review, local-first validation, and direct notes about what still needs to improve. The app has evolved through bathymetry intake, scale coherence, debug overlays, runtime shader diagnostics, twilight calibration, and the R0012 atmosphere LUT solver.
